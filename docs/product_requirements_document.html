## Project Brief: Profumeria Wanda - Brand Logo Refresh & Modernization **Project Title:** Profumeria Wanda - Brand Logo Refresh & Modernization **Version:** 1.0 **Date:** October 26, 2023 **Prepared By:** [Your Name/Team] --- ### 1. Introduction & Background Profumeria Wanda, a historic boutique established in 1960, is seeking to refresh its brand identity. The goal is to develop new logo concepts that honor the brand's rich heritage and warm, personal approach while integrating modern design aesthetics. The existing brand assets require an update to better reflect the sophisticated, artisanal nature of its perfume and leather goods offerings and to align with contemporary visual standards. ### 2. Project Goals * To develop compelling new logo concepts that modernize the Profumeria Wanda brand identity. * To ensure the new logos successfully blend the brand's historical legacy ("dal 1960") with a sophisticated, current aesthetic. * To explore diverse thematic directions (Sophisticated Heritage, Artisanal Emblem, Personal Signature) to identify the optimal visual representation for the brand's evolving image. * To provide distinct and versatile logo options suitable for various digital and potential print applications. ### 3. Target Audience * **Existing Loyal Customers:** Individuals who appreciate Profumeria Wanda's long-standing history, personalized service, and curated selection. * **New & Younger Patrons:** Consumers seeking high-quality, artisanal perfumes and leather goods in a refined, unique boutique setting. * **Values:** Customers who value tradition, craftsmanship, exclusivity, and a bespoke shopping experience. ### 4. Scope of Work **In Scope:** * Development and presentation of three distinct logo concept variants based on the identified design directions. * Detailed descriptions and strategic rationale for each proposed logo variant. * Suggestion of primary color palettes and thematic interpretations for each concept. * Initial design consideration for desktop web orientation. **Out of Scope (for this phase):** * Development of a comprehensive brand style guide. * Application of logos to all specific marketing materials (e.g., full website integration, signage, packaging). * Extensive market research or user testing. * Final selection of the definitive logo (this phase is for concept approval). ### 5. Key Deliverables * **Logo Variant 1 Concept:** "Sophisticated Heritage" (description and implied visual). * **Logo Variant 2 Concept:** "Artisanal Emblem" (description and implied visual). * **Logo Variant 3 Concept:** "Personal Signature" (description and implied visual). * A concise summary document detailing each proposed logo, including its core theme, proposed typography, suggested iconography, and color scheme. ### 6. Design Principles & Requirements The new logo concepts must adhere to the following principles: * **Heritage Integration:** Clearly convey the brand's establishment date and long-standing presence ("dal 1960"). * **Warmth & Personalization:** Evoke the intimate, bespoke experience of a boutique atelier. * **Sophistication & Elegance:** Reflect the high-quality nature of perfumes and leather goods. * **Modernity:** Incorporate contemporary design elements to ensure timelessness and appeal to current aesthetics. * **Versatility:** Be adaptable for various applications across digital and print media. * **Thematic Alignment:** Incorporate elements related to perfume and/or leather goods where appropriate. ### 7. Proposed Logo Concepts Three distinct logo variants have been developed to address the project goals: #### 7.1. Logo Variant: Sophisticated Heritage * **Description:** An elegant and modern serif typography for the brand name "Wanda." The founding year "dal 1960" will be rendered in a smaller, clean sans-serif font positioned underneath. A subtle, stylized flower or perfume droplet icon will be cleverly integrated into the 'W' of "Wanda." * **Theme:** Emphasizes timeless elegance, brand legacy, and refined tradition. * **Colors:** Deep Fuchsia (as a primary accent) and Soft Cream (for background or secondary elements). * **Orientation:** Designed with desktop web display in mind. #### 7.2. Logo Variant: Artisanal Emblem * **Description:** A distinctive circular emblem logo. Within the circle, a minimalist, hand-drawn illustration will feature silhouettes of a perfume bottle and a leather bag, signifying the core offerings. The text "Wanda - Il Nostro Atelier" will gracefully wrap around the inner or outer perimeter of the circle. * **Theme:** Highlights craftsmanship, personalized service, and the unique "atelier" experience. * **Colors:** Warm Terracotta (suggesting natural materials like leather) and Fuchsia (for a touch of luxury). * **Orientation:** Designed with desktop web display in mind. #### 7.3. Logo Variant: Personal Signature * **Description:** A signature-style script font for "Wanda" that conveys a personal, handwritten feel, promoting warmth and approachability. This will be paired with a clean, geometric sans-serif font for the descriptive text "PROFUMERIA & PELLETTERIA." A small accent icon, such as a heart or a sparkle, will be subtly placed near the end of the script for a charming touch. * **Theme:** Focuses on the unique, personal connection and bespoke service offered by the brand. * **Colors:** Fuchsia (as a vibrant accent) on a light, neutral background. * **Orientation:** Designed with desktop web display in mind. ### 8. Success Metrics * Client approval and selection of one or more logo concepts for further refinement. * Positive feedback from key stakeholders regarding how well the concepts align with desired brand attributes and aesthetic appeal. * The chosen logo effectively communicates Profumeria Wanda's heritage, sophistication, and personal touch. ### 9. Next Steps 1. **Client Review:** Presentation of these three logo concepts to Profumeria Wanda stakeholders for feedback and discussion. 2. **Concept Selection/Refinement:** Based on feedback, selection of a preferred concept(s) for further development and iterative design. 3. **Application (Future Phase):** Exploration of applying the chosen logo to key brand touchpoints (e.g., website homepage, business cards, social media profiles). 4. **Brand Guidelines (Future Phase):** Development of comprehensive brand guidelines to ensure consistent usage across all platforms.
